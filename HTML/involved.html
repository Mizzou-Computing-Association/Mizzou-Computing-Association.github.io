Get Involved!

SIGs

Every semester MCA host a variety of different special interest groups (SIGs). Each SIG is lead by a student and is for members to learn more about a specific area in technology. If you are interest in leading a SIG, please reach out to the current SIG Director on Slack/Discord!


TigerHacks

Want to show off your latest projects or learn new skills not taught in class? This is the place for you! From web development, to data science, to mobile development, our whiteboards and special interest groups (SIGs) can help you hit the ground running in a number of new areas.
